About Scott Rouse

Scott Rouse is a dedicated father, professional, and community member running for the Hellgate Elementary School Board. With a strong background in leadership, technology, and problem-solving, he is committed to ensuring academic excellence and responsible governance for our schools.

Inspired by his father, John Rouse, a lifelong educator, Scott is determined to uphold the same dedication to education and student success.

Campaign Priorities

  • Enhancing Student Success: Ensuring access to strong academics, modern learning tools, and future-ready education.
  • Supporting Teachers & Staff: Advocating for competitive salaries, professional development, and policies that allow teachers to focus on teaching.
  • Strengthening School-Community Partnerships: Promoting transparency, fiscal responsibility, and engagement between parents, educators, and the district.
